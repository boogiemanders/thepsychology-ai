## Where Mometrix Does Well

**Free practice test.** Mometrix offers a free EPPP practice test online. If you want a quick baseline with zero commitment, that's useful.

**Multiple formats.** Study guide (physical book or PDF), flashcards, online course. Some people like having a book they can highlight.

**Affordable.** Their products are reasonably priced compared to AATBS and PsychPrep.

**Brand recognition.** Mometrix is well-known in test prep broadly. If you've taken other professional exams, you may already be familiar with their format.

---

## Where Mometrix Falls Short

**Generic test prep factory.** This is the core issue. Mometrix creates study materials for hundreds of exams. Their EPPP content is created by the same process that produces their nursing, teaching, and real estate exam materials. The depth of psychology-specific expertise is limited.

**Definition-based questions.** Mometrix questions tend to test recall: "What is X?" The real EPPP asks you to apply concepts to clinical scenarios. Practicing recall questions builds false confidence about your readiness.

**Static materials.** A study guide doesn't adapt to your performance. Neither do flashcards. You study the same material regardless of what you already know.

**No adaptive practice.** The free practice test is a fixed set of questions. It doesn't adjust, doesn't target weak areas, doesn't evolve as you study.

**No audio.** Text-only learning. No audio lessons, no multimodal study options.

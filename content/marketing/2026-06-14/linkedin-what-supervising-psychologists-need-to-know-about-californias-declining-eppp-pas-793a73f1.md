## Draft

Supervisors are the most attuned observers of what is actually happening with EPPP prep. That perspective is almost entirely absent from the policy conversation.

Here is what every supervising psychologist in California should know right now.

California's 2025 EPPP pass rate was 36.5 percent overall. First-time candidates passed at 58.7 percent. Both figures are trending lower than any year from 2021 to 2024 (California Board of Psychology, 2026).

The ASPPB abandoned its planned EPPP Part 2 exam. The current exam is unchanged for anyone testing now or in the near future. The earliest any eligibility rule changes could take effect is 2027, and nothing is finalized.

What is worth your attention: the pass rate decline. It is not explained by candidates being less prepared in theory. The data points to a method problem more than a knowledge problem.

Are you actively helping your postdocs build a targeted study plan, or leaving that entirely to prep programs?

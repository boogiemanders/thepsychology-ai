Most people assume dark triad traits show up as deficits or brain damage. They don't.

## VALUE [8-38s]
A 2025 study used machine learning on resting-state brain scans from 200 participants. They tracked which brain networks stay active at baseline.

Dark triad traits correlated with elevated activity in the central executive network. That's the system for goal maintenance, focused attention, and flexible problem solving.

So the dark triad brain is not a broken brain. It is a calibrated one, oriented toward achieving its objectives.

## PAYOFF [38-44s]
Machiavellian traits specifically linked to more activity in that goal-directed system. What looks like manipulation may actually be strategy. That distinction matters clinically.

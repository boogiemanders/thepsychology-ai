# What 207,000 people found about forgiveness

**TikTok script · Pop Culture**

## Draft

Everyone says forgive and move on. Researchers just tested whether that is actually true. 207,000 people across 23 countries.

This is not self-help. This is peer-reviewed research. And the findings are more complicated than you expect.

People who consistently forgave others reported better psychological well-being one year later. Higher optimism. Stronger relationships.

But here is the nuance. The benefits were psychological, not physical. And the effect size was modest.

The impact also varied by country. Forgiveness did not uniformly help everyone equally.

What this means clinically: forgiveness tends to protect you, not the other person. The burden you release is yours. That is why it works.

# What Supervisors Need to Know About the EPPP2 Right Now

**LinkedIn post · EPPP**

## Draft

Supervisors are the first people their postdocs ask about the EPPP2. Here is what the actual record shows.

The short version: the exam your supervisee is studying for today is unchanged. The EPPP2 was paused in October 2024 and later abandoned entirely. No confirmed launch date exists.

The California Board of Psychology reviewed all of this at its February 2026 meeting. The earliest window being named for any new integrated exam is 2027, and nothing is confirmed (California Board of Psychology, 2026).

Three things worth knowing if a supervisee asks today:

1. The EPPP2 is not happening in 2026. That was settled in late 2024.
2. A 2027 launch has been discussed at the board level. Nothing is finalized.
3. California's first-time pass rate in 2025 was 58.7%. The exam that exists is real, and the stakes are real.

The most useful thing a supervisor can do right now is redirect postdocs to the exam in front of them, not the one that was proposed and shelved.

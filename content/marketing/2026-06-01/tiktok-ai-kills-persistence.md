# A 2026 study found AI makes you more likely to give up on hard problems. It only takes 10 minutes.

**TikTok script · Psychology**

## Draft

[HOOK]
A 2026 study found that using AI for just 10 minutes made people more likely to give up when working alone.

[BEAT: phone or laptop visual on screen]
Researchers ran a randomized controlled trial with 1,222 participants. Math problems. Reading comprehension. Standard tasks. They split people into an AI group and a control group.

The AI group performed better while the AI was on.

Then the researchers took it away.

[BEAT: visual cut, screen goes blank]
The AI group performed significantly worse than the control group on the same tasks. And they quit faster.

[BEAT: face cam, direct address]
Here is why this matters.

AI conditions you to expect an immediate answer. When one does not come, your brain reads that as a signal to stop. The friction of not knowing something yet is where learning actually happens. When AI removes that friction, you stop building the muscle.

Persistence is one of the strongest predictors of long-term skill development. That is not a motivational claim. That is what the data says.

[BEAT: text overlay on screen]
This is not anti-AI. It is about sequence.

Use AI to check your thinking, not to replace it.

[BEAT: CTA, direct to camera]
If you are studying for a high-stakes exam, this is the one thing to protect. Do the hard reps without the safety net first.

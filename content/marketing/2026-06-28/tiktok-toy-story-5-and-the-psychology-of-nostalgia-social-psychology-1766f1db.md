BUILD-UP
When you feel nostalgia, you are not just remembering. You are doing something specific with that memory.
PAYOFF
Nostalgia is not just a memory. It is a motivational state that uses memory to serve a present need. Research found that nostalgia increases optimism, strengthens the sense of social connection, and links your past self to your present identity. That is called self-continuity.
That is why Buzz and Woody feel different on a second viewing as an adult. They are not just characters. They are anchors to a version of you that felt secure and connected.

# Music producers vs. AI: the IKEA effect

## Draft

## HOOK (0-3s)
Every music producer is fine using AI. Except when it does this one thing.

## BUILD-UP (3-8s)
And before you assume it's about copyright, it's not about royalties. It's not about piracy.

## VALUE (8-35s)
A survey of over 1,100 producers found nearly three out of five are fine with AI doing the boring work. Noise reduction. Vocal tuning. File cleanup.

But the moment AI tries to write the melody, pick the sounds, or make an aesthetic call? They push back hard.

There's a psychology concept called the IKEA effect. When you build something yourself, you value it more. Not because it's better. Because it's yours.

When AI makes the creative decision, the output stops feeling like yours. The ownership disappears.

## PAYOFF
Producers aren't anti-AI. They're pro-authorship. That is the most human response possible.

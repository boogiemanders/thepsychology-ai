# Your postdocs may be delaying their EPPP. Here is what the Board documents say.

**LinkedIn post · EPPP**

## Draft

Postdocs in your cohort are probably delaying their EPPP right now. The reason they give is the exam change.

Here is what the California Board of Psychology's February 2026 meeting materials actually say.

ASPPB built a two-part exam plan. Part 2 was supposed to launch January 1, 2026. The Board approved rules in 2023.

In October 2024, ASPPB paused the plan. Then abandoned it entirely.

The EPPP your supervisees are studying for right now is unchanged. No Part 2. No new skills exam (California Board of Psychology, 2026).

2027 is the earliest possible window for any structural change, and that timeline is not confirmed.

California's first-time pass rate was 58.7% in 2025, lower than prior years (California Board of Psychology, 2026). The exam is hard. Waiting for a change that has not been confirmed costs candidates real momentum.

What are you telling your postdocs about the exam changes right now?

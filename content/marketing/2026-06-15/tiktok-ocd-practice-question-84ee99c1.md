# OCD Practice Question

**TikTok script · EPPP**

## Draft

**HOOK**
U-C-L-A trained psychologist. Here is the E triple P practice question most candidates miss.

**BUILD-UP**
The answer is going to feel wrong at first.

**QUESTION**
A client with O-C-D washes their hands 50 times a day to reduce contamination anxiety. The first-line evidence-based treatment is:

A, systematic desensitization.
B, exposure and response prevention.
C, C-B-T with thought challenging.
D, relaxation training.

**WRONG ANSWERS**
A is wrong because systematic desensitization targets phobia responses with a relaxation hierarchy, not compulsion cycles.
C is wrong because thought challenging alone does not stop compulsive behavior.
D is wrong because relaxation reinforces avoidance.

**PAYOFF**
The answer is B. Exposure and response prevention.

O-C-D is maintained by the compulsion, not just the thought. E-R-P works by exposing the client to the feared trigger and blocking the compulsive response. Anxiety rises, then drops through habituation. That is what breaks the cycle.

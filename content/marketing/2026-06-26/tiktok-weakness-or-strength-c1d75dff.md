# Weakness Or Strength?

**TikTok script · Psychology**

## Draft

HOOK (0-3s):
A 20-minute writing exercise helped people with depression make 49 percent more progress on their goals.

BUILD-UP (3-8s):
Not pop-psych. Peer-reviewed. Bauer, Walton, Hoyer, and Job published this in 2026. Three experiments, 748 participants.

VALUE (8-40s):
Participants who had experienced depression wrote a brief essay naming strengths their illness had built in them.

Two weeks later, they had made 64 percent progress on a valued personal goal. The control group: 43 percent.

The mechanism: the belief that depression makes you less capable suppresses motivation more than the depression itself does.

Change that belief, and they move.

PAYOFF:
One writing session. A measurable shift two weeks later. The story a client tells about their illness is not just context. It is intervention territory.

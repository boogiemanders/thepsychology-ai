## Draft

**[HOOK -- 0-3 sec]**
[Talking head, direct to camera, subtitles on]
If you called someone a narcissist this week, a licensed psychologist needs to correct the record.

**[BUILD-UP -- 3-18 sec]**
TikTok has turned clinical vocabulary into insults. Gaslighting. Narcissist. Trauma. Boundaries. These words have real clinical definitions. The internet version is something different.

**[VALUE -- 18-42 sec]**
Gaslighting is a specific manipulation tactic used to make someone question their own perception of reality. It is not your boss ignoring your email.

Narcissistic Personality Disorder has strict diagnostic criteria and affects a small portion of the population. It is not your ex being selfish.

Trauma has a clinical threshold. Not every bad experience meets it.

When these words lose precision, the people who actually experience these things get dismissed. Clinically, this matters. When vocabulary loses meaning, real suffering loses language.

**[PAYOFF -- 42-52 sec]**
TikTok taught you the words. It also broke them. Your clients are using this vocabulary. Know what they mean by it.

**[CTA -- 52-60 sec]**
Follow for psychology that sounds like a psychologist, not a comment section.

# The mandatory EPPP Part 2 has been cancelled. Here is what you need to know.

**LinkedIn post · EPPP**

> [!warning] Needs human review

## Draft

The ASPPB was developing a second mandatory exam to stack on top of the EPPP you are already studying for. A skills-based assessment, separate fee, separate test day.

Multiple states pushed back hard. Texas filed an FTC antitrust complaint and started building its own alternative licensing exam. The mandatory Part 2 requirement is off the table.

If you are currently in EPPP prep, your path is the same one it was when you started. No surprise second exam. Same 8 domains. Same passing standard.

What this does not change: the EPPP itself is still real. The roughly 24% fail rate is still real. The exam you are studying for is the exam you will take.

If you have been delaying your prep, waiting to see what the licensing landscape would look like, you have your answer now. Start.

The noise is over. The EPPP is the EPPP.

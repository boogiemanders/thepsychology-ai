# The EPPP Part 2 is gone. Here is what the 2027 window actually means.

**LinkedIn post · EPPP**

## Draft

Everyone has heard a different version of what happened to the EPPP Part 2.

Here is what the California Board of Psychology materials actually say.

ASPPB abandoned the two-part exam after pausing it in October 2024. The Board had approved EPPP Part 2 rules in 2023, built rule packages around them, then had to reopen that work when ASPPB pulled out.

The EPPP you are studying for right now is unchanged.

The earliest possible implementation window for any new integrated exam is 2027. That is not a launch date. That is the earliest the Board discussed as a possibility. Nothing is confirmed.

One number worth knowing: California's first-time EPPP pass rate was 58.7% in 2025. The overall pass rate was 36.5%. That gap is not random. First-time candidates who prepare the right way are on the better side of it.

The confusion around the EPPP2 has cost candidates real mental energy. The answer is straightforward: the exam in front of you today is the same exam it has always been. Prepare for that one.

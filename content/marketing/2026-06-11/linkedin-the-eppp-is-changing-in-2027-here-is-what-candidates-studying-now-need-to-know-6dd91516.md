# The EPPP is changing in 2027. Here is what candidates studying now need to know.

**LinkedIn post · EPPP**

## Draft

The EPPP is getting a major overhaul in 2027. Here is what candidates studying now actually need to know.

In February 2026, ASPPB approved the content specifications for a new integrated exam (ASPPB, 2026). Single-session. Computer-based. Tests both knowledge and skills in the same sitting.

New question types: scenario-based, audio, video.

The timeline:
- Sample test: Fall 2026
- Beta administration: March 2027
- Fully operational: Fall 2027

What is not changing yet: the EPPP you are studying for today is the same exam it has always been (California Board of Psychology, 2026). Keep preparing. Just know what is coming.

One thing still being debated: ASPPB originally wanted candidates to finish all post-doctoral hours before testing. That eligibility rule has not been finalized (California Board of Psychology, 2026).

If you are eligible now and close to ready, the calculation is simple: test before 2027.

# The EPPP is not changing in 2026. Here is what is actually happening.

**LinkedIn post · EPPP**

## Draft

The EPPP is not changing in 2026.

Here is what actually happened.

ASPPB originally planned a two-part exam: a knowledge section and a separate skills section, set to launch January 2026. That plan was abandoned.

What they are now building is a single integrated exam that combines knowledge and skills. ASPPB released content specifications in February 2026. The earliest this integrated exam launches is Q4 2027.

If you are studying for the EPPP right now, you are preparing for the current, unchanged exam.

California's first-time pass rate in 2025 was 58.7%. That is where your attention belongs. Not the format of an exam that does not exist yet.

The most common reason candidates fail has nothing to do with exam format. It is content gaps in 2 or 3 specific domains.

Study the current exam. Pass it. Then watch what ASPPB announces in 2027.

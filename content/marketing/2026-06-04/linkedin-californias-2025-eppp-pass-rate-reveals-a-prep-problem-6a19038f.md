# California's 2025 EPPP pass rate reveals a prep problem

**LinkedIn post · EPPP**

## Draft

California's 2025 EPPP pass rate was 36.5%.

First-time candidates passed at 58.7%.

That 22-point gap is worth sitting with.

The overall rate is dragged down by repeat attempts. A lot of candidates are sitting this exam more than once. And according to the California Board of Psychology's February 2026 meeting data, 2025 pass rates are trending lower than any year from 2021 through 2024.

This is not a commentary on intelligence or preparation level. It points to something more fixable: method.

The same content, the same exam, but repeat test-takers are failing again on average. Which means studying more of the same thing is not the solution.

The prep approach that gives you the best odds on attempt one is also the most efficient: diagnostic-first, quiz-heavy, targeted to your actual gaps. Not a uniform grind through all eight domains.

# What to tell your postdoc about the EPPP2

**LinkedIn post · EPPP**

## Draft

Your postdoc is probably carrying anxiety about an exam that does not exist yet.

ASPPB paused the EPPP2 in October 2024 and later abandoned it. The California Board of Psychology reviewed the fallout at their February 2026 meeting. The earliest any new exam could appear is 2027, and that timeline is not confirmed (California Board of Psychology, 2026).

The exam candidates are studying for today is unchanged. There is no second hurdle this year.

What this means practically: candidates who have been delaying their test date out of fear of the EPPP2 are waiting for something with no firm launch date.

Most of what is circulating in online study forums about the EPPP2 is outdated or speculative. Supervisors are often the first source candidates trust on exam questions.

If you supervise postdocs: what EPPP2 questions are you fielding right now?

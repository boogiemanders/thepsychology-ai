# Variable Ratio Reinforcement: Which schedule is hardest to extinguish?

**TikTok script · EPPP**

## Draft

Hardest To Quit

The E triple P asks: which reinforcement schedule produces the highest and most persistent response rate?

Four choices.

Fixed interval is wrong because reinforcement arrives after a predictable time period. The behavior pauses right before the reward, then spikes.

Variable interval is wrong because it varies the time between rewards, not the number of responses needed to earn one.

Fixed ratio is wrong because reinforcement comes after a fixed number of responses. Predictable enough that responding actually pauses right after each reward.

Variable ratio is correct. Reinforcement comes after an unpredictable number of responses.

That unpredictability produces the highest response rate.

You never know when the next reward arrives, so you never stop responding.

This is why gambling disorders are so hard to treat. The schedule is doing the work, not the person.

# The EPPP is getting 6 new content domains. Here is what supervisors need to know now.

**LinkedIn post · EPPP**

## Draft

In December 2025, ASPPB approved a new content blueprint for the EPPP, operational in Q4 2027.

The current 8 domains are being reorganized into 6: Scientific Orientation to Practice, Assessment, Intervention, Consultation and Supervision, Interpersonal Relationships, and Ethical and Professional Practice.

Format changes too: single-session, multiple-choice plus extended scenarios and audio and video components.

None of this affects candidates testing before Q4 2027. The exam your postdoc is sitting today is unchanged (California Board of Psychology, 2026).

But if they are 18 months or more from their test date, they may be studying for a blueprint that no longer exists when they sit.

The transition window is the variable most supervisors are not tracking.

If you supervise a postdoctoral trainee right now, do you know which version they will sit?

# The EPPP2 is gone. Here is what people studying right now need to know.

**LinkedIn post · EPPP**

## Draft

The EPPP2 is not coming.

ASPPB paused it in October 2024. Then abandoned it.

California Board of Psychology reviewed the fallout at its February 2026 meeting (California Board of Psychology, 2026).

Here is what the data shows right now:

Overall California EPPP pass rate in 2025: 36.5%.
First-time candidates: 58.7%.

Roughly 4 in 10 first-timers did not pass. The cause is almost always one of three things:
→ Content gaps in 2 or 3 domains
→ Passive study methods (reading instead of quizzing)
→ Test anxiety reducing performance below actual knowledge level

Each has a different fix. None is "wait to see what happens with the exam format."

2027 is the earliest possible window for any change to the exam format. The exam you are preparing for today is unchanged.

Study for the test in front of you.

## VALUE (8-50s)

"Here is what a high-yield EPPP study hour actually looks like.

First: 20 to 25 minutes of practice questions. Not reading. Questions.

Second: for every wrong answer, write down why you missed it. Content gap? Misread the question? Eliminated the correct option? Those are three different problems with three different fixes.

Third: only then go read the relevant section for the gap you just found.

You are not studying to feel productive. You are studying to close the gaps the exam will test.

Candidates who shift to this approach often cut their weekly study hours while their scores improve."

[ACTIONABLE STEPS -- 3 clear, followable steps]
[EXTREME VALUE -- protocol a candidate can implement today]

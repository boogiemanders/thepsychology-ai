## Draft

A psychologist explains what it actually means when science says an AI chatbot works.

NEJM AI just published the first randomized controlled trial showing a generative AI chatbot reduced symptoms of clinical depression and anxiety.

This is significant. But here is what one RCT actually proves.

An RCT tells you the effect was real and unlikely due to chance in this sample. It does not tell you it will replicate across different populations, different settings, or different versions of the chatbot. It does not make the treatment evidence-based.

The American Psychological Association's criteria for a well-established treatment require at least two independent replications with strong designs. One study is not enough.

The research is moving in the right direction. The coverage is not catching up.

# EPPP Test-Day Strategy: The Two-Pass System

**TikTok script · EPPP Strategy**

## Draft

Here is the strategy I used to get through 225 E triple P questions without running out of time. I call it the two-pass system.

The exam gives you 255 minutes for 225 questions. That is about 68 seconds per item. Do not try to solve every hard question on the first pass.

Pass one: move at 45 to 60 seconds per item. If you do not have a confident answer in that window, flag it and keep moving. Never leave a blank.

Pass two: return to flagged questions with whatever time remains.

Here is why this works. Getting stuck on one hard question costs you the time you needed for three easier ones. That is the exam's trap. Anchoring on a question you cannot answer is a decision, and it is the wrong one. The two-pass system keeps you in the driver's seat.

I used this on my actual exam and finished with time left to review.

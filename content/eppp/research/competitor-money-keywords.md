# Competitors to thePsychology.ai and Their Money-Making Keywords

ThePsychology.ai sells practice questions and study help for the Examination for Professional Practice in Psychology (EPPP). EPPP is a test psychologists must pass to get a licence. Because of this, the site competes with companies that also offer EPPP exam prep, practice tests, and study guides. Below are the main competitors and the keywords they use to get customers. These "money keywords" are phrases that people type into search engines when they are ready to buy study materials.

## Main competitors and their keywords

### 1. Association for Advanced Training in the Behavioral Sciences (AATBS)
**What they do:** Long-running company that sells EPPP exam prep packages and study materials. Their site lists separate sections for "EPPP Exam Prep," "EPPP Study Materials," and even a second exam (EPPP Part 2).

**Money keywords:** Because their menu points to "EPPP Exam Prep" and "EPPP Study Materials", AATBS likely ranks for phrases such as:
- eppp exam prep
- eppp study materials
- eppp practice exams
- cplee exam prep (they also sell CPLEE materials)
- licensure exam prep

### 2. PsychPrep
**What they do:** PsychPrep offers a range of EPPP programs (basic, premium, workshops) and emphasises personalised study help. A site-analysis page lists a long set of meta-keywords for PsychPrep.

**Money keywords:** Their meta-tag shows exactly which phrases they target:
- psychprep (brand name)
- eppp / eppp exam
- eppp study materials
- eppp practice tests
- eppp sample questions
- psychology licensure exam prep
- test preparation
- eppp workshops / online workshops
- cpse and cplee (other psychology licensure exams)

These keywords show that PsychPrep wants to rank for people looking for exam prep, study materials and practice tests.

### 3. PrepJet
**What they do:** PrepJet markets itself as a streamlined EPPP study program with a free trial. A site-analysis page lists their meta keywords as "prepjet, eppp, mft".

**Money keywords:** From the meta-keywords and their focus on law and ethics exam prep, likely profitable search terms include:
- prepjet (brand)
- eppp prep / eppp study program
- mft exam prep
- mft law and ethics exam

### 4. Taylor Study Method
**What they do:** Offers customised online EPPP and CPLEE study plans. Their meta-keywords list "EPPP Licensing Exam Prep, EPPP Prep, EPPP Study".

**Money keywords:** Based on their meta-keywords:
- eppp licensing exam prep
- eppp prep
- eppp study
- cplee exam prep

### 5. Study.com
**What they do:** An educational platform offering online courses, video lessons and practice tests for many exams. Its EPPP page says that Study.com provides video lessons, practice questions and diagnostic tests for the EPPP.

**Money keywords:** People looking for these services often search for:
- eppp online course
- eppp practice tests
- eppp video lessons
- eppp study schedule
- eppp test prep subscription

### 6. Mometrix
**What they do:** A large test-prep company that offers a free EPPP practice test, an online course, a study guide and flashcards. Their EPPP practice-test page highlights links to an "EPPP Online Course," "EPPP Study Guide," and "EPPP Flashcards".

**Money keywords:** The navigation and headings indicate that Mometrix targets:
- eppp practice test
- eppp online course
- eppp study guide
- eppp flashcards
- free eppp practice test

### 7. AlphaPrep (AlphaPrep.ai)
**What they do:** A newer competitor that markets "affordable, interactive EPPP exam prep for everyone" and holds workshops on how to use large language models (LLMs) for the EPPP.

**Money keywords:** Their tagline suggests keywords like:
- affordable eppp prep
- interactive eppp exam prep
- ai eppp prep
- eppp workshops

### 8. Other providers
- **Academic Review:** Offers EPPP study materials and is often mentioned in comparison tables. Likely keywords: eppp study materials, academic review eppp, and affordable eppp prep.
- **Gerry Grossman Seminars:** Provides exam prep for psychology licences. Keywords likely include gerry grossman eppp and psychology exam prep.
- **Dr. David's EPPP** and similar smaller companies sell practice tests; their keywords centre on eppp practice tests and eppp exam questions.

## Cross-competitor keyword themes

Across all competitors there are certain phrases that people search for when they want to buy EPPP study products. These high-value keywords include:

- **EPPP exam prep / EPPP prep course** - almost every competitor uses this phrase in navigation menus or meta tags.
- **EPPP study materials / study guide** - found in AATBS menus and Mometrix page links.
- **EPPP practice test / practice questions** - emphasised by Mometrix's free practice test and PsychPrep's keywords.
- **EPPP online course / online program** - a key product for Study.com, Mometrix and Taylor Study Method.
- **EPPP sample questions / flashcards** - offered by PsychPrep and Mometrix.
- **CPLEE / CPSE / MFT** - alternative psychology licensure exams targeted by PsychPrep, PrepJet and other sites.
- **Affordable EPPP prep** - emphasised by AlphaPrep and is a growing niche.

## Summary

Competitors to thePsychology.ai are other companies that sell EPPP exam preparation, practice tests and study guides. The biggest names are AATBS, PsychPrep, PrepJet, Taylor Study Method, Study.com, Mometrix, AlphaPrep and Academic Review. They make money by ranking for keywords related to EPPP exam prep, study materials, practice tests, online courses and other licensure exams. Knowing which keywords drive traffic can help thePsychology.ai position its own content and advertising to attract test-takers.

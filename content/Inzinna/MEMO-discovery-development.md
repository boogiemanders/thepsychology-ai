## DISCOVERY, DEVELOPMENT, AND INTERNAL USE MEMO

**Date:** [DATE]
**Between:** Inzinna Psychology Group ("Inzinna") and Anders Chan

---

### Purpose

This memo clarifies how the project work will be owned and used so both parties can move forward confidently. The goal is simple: Inzinna gets durable internal-use rights to everything built for the practice, and Anders retains the underlying systems and the right to develop a broader product outside of Inzinna. This memo is a working agreement, not a comprehensive legal contract — if either party wants to do more, a separate agreement will cover that.

---

### 1. Project scope

This project covers workflow automation, assessment scoring, development of an executive functioning assessment tool, and possible payroll workflow optimization. The current phase includes:

- discovery meetings with Greg, Carlos, Bret, and other relevant stakeholders
- a needs-assessment survey completed by the team to define current pain points and priorities
- identifying workflow needs and scoping deliverables based on survey results
- designing systems, workflows, tools, scoring methods, templates, or prototypes
- developing deliverables related to assessment scoring, executive functioning tools, and payroll workflow
- preparing recommendations, drafts, or implementation materials

---

### 2. Role clarification

Anders Chan's employment role at Inzinna is Director of Training and AI Education at $90/hour. That role covers training, education programming, and AI-related instruction within the practice.

The work described in this memo — tool development, assessment design, workflow automation, and product development — is a separate project and is not treated as part of Anders Chan's ordinary employment duties under that role unless both parties agree in writing that a specific task belongs there.

If any task is later agreed to fall within Anders Chan's regular employment duties, that will be confirmed in writing before the work begins.

---

### 3. Current status and access

As of the date of this memo, no tools, extensions, or systems developed by Anders Chan have been shared with or deployed for Inzinna Psychology Group.

Access to those tools begins only after:

1. This memo is signed by both parties, and
2. Where the tools involve patient data, a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) is in place

Work already developed prior to this memo — including but not limited to the SimplePractice Notes Chrome extension and the ZocDoc-to-SimplePractice extension — is subject to the ownership framework in Sections 6 and 7 below. Inzinna-specific implementations fall under Inzinna's internal-use license. The underlying systems, logic, and generalized framework remain Anders Chan's property.

---

### 4. Deliverables

Anticipated deliverables include some or all of the following, to be finalized based on the needs-assessment survey results:

- a method or system for automating assessment scoring
- an executive functioning assessment tool
- recommendations, workflow maps, logic, drafts, prototypes, or related materials for payroll workflow efficiency
- internal implementation materials for Inzinna Psychology Group

Each deliverable will be defined clearly before completion-based payment is expected.

---

### 5. Compensation

Greg has proposed compensating Anders Chan based on completion of defined tasks or deliverables rather than hourly billing for this project.

- Compensation will be tied to clearly defined tasks, milestones, or deliverables agreed upon before the work begins
- If a task has not yet been clearly defined, the parties will define it before expecting payment
- If hourly compensation is preferred for any portion, that can be agreed separately in writing

---

### 6. Inzinna-specific materials

"Inzinna-specific materials" means materials created specifically for Inzinna Psychology Group or tailored to its internal workflows, operations, staff, or office needs. This includes:

- office-specific workflow maps and implementation notes
- office-specific scoring workflows
- custom versions of prompts, dashboards, forms, templates, or instructions built for Inzinna's internal use
- materials tied specifically to Inzinna's practice operations

---

### 7. Anders Chan's background IP and generalized framework

Anders Chan retains full ownership of all background intellectual property and all broader reusable systems, including:

- reusable templates, prompts, and prompt systems
- workflow logic and scoring logic
- code architecture and automation methods
- generalized assessment concepts, frameworks, or systems
- reusable product concepts and generalized tools that are not specific to Inzinna Psychology Group

This includes the right to continue developing, licensing, and commercializing the generalized version of any tool or system outside of Inzinna Psychology Group.

---

### 8. What Inzinna gets

Inzinna Psychology Group will have a perpetual, non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use the delivered materials for its own internal business operations. This means:

- Inzinna can continue using the delivered materials after this project ends, even if Anders Chan is no longer at the practice
- Inzinna can hire another developer or contractor to maintain, adapt, or modify the delivered materials for its own internal use
- These rights do not expire and do not require ongoing involvement from Anders Chan

---

### 9. Scope of Inzinna's rights

Inzinna's rights cover internal use of the delivered materials. Unless both parties sign a separate written agreement, Inzinna's rights do not include:

- selling the delivered materials outside the practice
- sublicensing or distributing them to other practices
- commercializing them as a product
- claiming ownership over Anders Chan's broader reusable framework, systems, logic, or generalized product layer

If Inzinna wants rights beyond internal use, that will be handled in a separate written agreement.

---

### 10. Future changes and support

Anders Chan is not required to provide future updates, changes, or support after his involvement on this project ends, unless separately agreed and compensated. Inzinna may use another developer for internal maintenance or modification of the delivered materials.

---

### 11. Commercialization outside Inzinna

Internal use by Inzinna Psychology Group is separate from participation in outside commercialization.

If Greg or Inzinna Psychology Group wants to participate in commercialization beyond internal use — including revenue share, equity, co-ownership, or licensing — that will be handled in a separate agreement with clearly defined terms and concrete commitments from both parties.

Nothing in this memo creates any commercialization rights for Inzinna Psychology Group beyond internal use of the delivered materials.

---

### 12. Executive functioning assessment tool

The executive functioning assessment tool is one of the anticipated deliverables. Ownership and commercialization rights follow the framework in this memo: Inzinna receives internal-use rights to the delivered implementation; Anders Chan retains ownership of the underlying generalized framework and the right to commercialize it outside the practice.

Nothing in this memo makes the assessment tool or its underlying framework the exclusive property of Inzinna Psychology Group unless both parties expressly agree to that in a separate written agreement.

---

### 13. Confidentiality and patient data

Each party will keep the other's non-public business information confidential.

No protected health information (PHI) or patient-identifiable information will be used in development or testing unless the required HIPAA documentation is in place — specifically, a signed Business Associate Agreement (BAA) covering Anders Chan's access to PHI in connection with this project. Until a BAA is signed, all development, testing, and demonstrations will use dummy, de-identified, or non-patient data.

Any tools developed under this project that involve PHI will not be used with real patient data until the BAA is executed.

---

### 14. No implied transfer

Except for the internal-use rights expressly granted to Inzinna Psychology Group in this memo, no ownership transfer, assignment, exclusive license, or commercialization right is created unless both parties sign a more specific written agreement.

---

### 15. End of project or separation

If Anders Chan leaves Inzinna Psychology Group or this project ends:

- Inzinna may continue using the delivered Inzinna-specific materials internally under the license in Section 8
- Anders Chan retains ownership of his background IP and generalized reusable framework
- Future changes or added development work will require a separate paid arrangement unless Inzinna engages another developer for internal support

---

### 16. Entire understanding for this project phase

This memo reflects the current understanding for this project phase. It may later be replaced or expanded by a more detailed agreement. Either party may propose changes in writing.

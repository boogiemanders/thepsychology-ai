## Implementation Notes

- Current lab BAARS config is still based on the local scorer workbook for raw scores and symptom counts.
- The norms PDF is the source needed to add age-banded percentile interpretation.
- To support percentile output in the app, the interpretation layer should:
  - detect form type: `current_symptoms` or `childhood_symptoms`
  - detect age band: `18-39`, `40-59`, or `60-89`
  - look up the raw score in the correct table
  - return the percentile row label that contains that raw score or range
- The scorer workbook alone is not enough for `40-59` and `60-89` percentile interpretation.

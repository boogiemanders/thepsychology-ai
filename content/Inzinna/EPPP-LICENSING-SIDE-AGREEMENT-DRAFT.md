## 0. Purpose and Relationship to Employment Agreement

This Side Agreement is the "separate written agreement" expressly required by Sections 9(i), 10.4, and 18 of the Employment Agreement v2.0 dated April 10, 2026 between the parties ("Employment Agreement"). ## 1. License Grant

Licensor grants Licensee a non-exclusive, non-transferable, royalty-bearing license to use the Licensed IP (defined below) to operate the EPPP Program (defined below) under Licensee's brand, for the Term.

### 1.1 Licensed IP
"Licensed IP" means Licensor's existing thePsychology.ai EPPP infrastructure, including:
- EPPP question bank and item metadata
- EPPP lessons, curriculum, and study-path content
- Quiz engine, spaced-repetition system, scoring/heatmap logic
- Underlying platform code, schemas, and AI prompt frameworks
- Any updates Licensor elects to share during the Term

The Licensed IP is Licensor's Prior IP under Schedule A of the Employment Agreement and remains Licensor's sole property.

### 1.2 EPPP Program
"EPPP Program" means the EPPP exam-preparation product, courses, CE offerings, seminars, workshops, coaching, and any related educational products or services that incorporate or are derived from the Licensed IP, marketed under Licensee's brand or co-brand.

### 1.3 What's Not Licensed
- Licensor's thePsychology.ai brand, domain, marketing site
- Licensor's customer list, email subscribers, or analytics data
- Any IP listed on Schedule A that is not within the Licensed IP scope above

## 2. Royalty

### 2.1 Rate
Licensee pays Licensor **thirty-five percent (35%) of Net Revenue** (defined below) generated by the EPPP Program.

### 2.2 Royalty Base
"Net Revenue" means gross revenue from the EPPP Program less only:
- Refunds actually paid
- Payment-processor fees actually charged (e.g., Stripe, PayPal)
- Sales tax actually collected and remitted

Royalty Base **expressly includes**:
- Prep-program subscription / one-time fees
- CE credit fees tied to or marketed alongside the EPPP Program
- Seminar, workshop, and live-event revenue tied to or marketed alongside the EPPP Program
- Coaching, tutoring, or supplemental services sold to EPPP Program customers
- Any bundles, upsells, or downstream products sold to EPPP Program customers within 24 months of their last EPPP Program purchase

Royalty Base **expressly excludes**:
- Licensee's marketing spend, salaries, overhead, rent, software, or other costs (these come out of Licensee's 65%, NOT off the top)

### 2.3 Payment
- Licensee pays royalties **monthly**, within 15 days after month-end
- Licensee provides a **monthly statement** showing gross revenue, deductions, Net Revenue, and royalty owed
- Licensor may **audit** Licensee's books once per calendar year, at Licensor's expense, with 14 days' notice. If an audit reveals underpayment of more than 5%, Licensee bears audit costs.

## 4. IP Ownership and No Assignment

- Licensor retains **all right, title, and interest** in the Licensed IP and any new IP created under this Side Agreement.
- This Side Agreement is a license **only**. No assignment, sale, or transfer of IP to Licensee is intended or effected.
- Licensee may use the Licensed IP only for the EPPP Program and only during the Term. On termination, Licensee's rights end (subject to a 90-day wind-down per Section 7.3).

## 5. Non-Exclusivity

- This license is **non-exclusive**. Licensor may continue to operate, sell, and grow thePsychology.ai's EPPP product under Licensor's own brand, to Licensor's own audience, on any terms Licensor chooses.
- Licensee will NOT claim that thePsychology.ai's independent EPPP product is "competing" with the EPPP Program in any way that would restrict Licensor's operation of thePsychology.ai.
- Licensor will NOT solicit Licensee's EPPP Program customers using customer data obtained through this Side Agreement. Licensor's general marketing of thePsychology.ai to the public is not a solicitation under this section.

### 5.3 Pricing and Anti-Undercut Floor
Licensee sets retail pricing for the EPPP Program at its sole discretion, subject to one floor: retail pricing for the prep program shall not be set below **eighty percent (80%) of Licensor's then-current public retail pricing** for the comparable thePsychology.ai EPPP product, without Licensor's written consent.

As of the Effective Date, Licensor's public retail price is **$30/month** for the comparable thePsychology.ai EPPP product, which sets the initial floor at **$24/month**. The floor adjusts automatically as Licensor's public price changes.

This floor does NOT apply to:
- Time-limited promotional pricing of 30 days or less
- Beta cohorts or early-access pilots
- Scholarship or hardship seats
- Institutional, B2B, or bulk-licensing pricing to organizations
- Bundled pricing where the EPPP Program is sold alongside other paid Licensee products, provided the implied EPPP Program price is reasonable

Licensee will give Licensor 14 days' written notice of any change to retail pricing.

## 7. Term, Termination, and Survival

### 7.1 Term
Initial term of **24 months** from the Effective Date, auto-renewing for successive 12-month terms unless either party provides 90 days' written notice of non-renewal.

### 7.2 Termination for cause
Either party may terminate immediately on material breach not cured within 30 days of written notice.

### 7.3 Wind-Down
On termination or non-renewal:
- Licensee has 90 days to wind down the EPPP Program (continue serving existing customers, no new sales)
- Royalty obligations continue through the wind-down period
- After wind-down, Licensee ceases all use of the Licensed IP and deletes derivative materials within 30 days

### 7.4 Survival of Royalty Beyond Postdoc
**Royalty obligations under this Side Agreement survive the termination, expiration, or non-renewal of the Employment Agreement.** Anders's status as Licensor under this Side Agreement is independent of Anders's status as Employee under the Employment Agreement. End of postdoc does not end the license or the royalty.

### 7.5 Sections That Survive
Sections 3 (10.2 Carve-Out), 4 (IP Ownership), 6 (Attribution), 7.4 (Survival), 9 (Reps), and 11 (Boilerplate) survive any termination.

## Base Message

**Subject:** Want to pilot the SimplePractice AI assistant?

Hi [Name],

Thanks again for filling out the clinician survey — your responses were some of the most useful data we got. Based on what you shared, I think you'd be a great fit to pilot the first version of the SimplePractice AI assistant we've been building at Inzinna.

Quick summary of what it does:

- One-click capture of intake + GAD-7/PHQ-9 from DIPS
- Auto-fills the 111-field ICE form from the intake
- Generates a SOAP note draft during and after session (runs locally on your laptop — nothing leaves your machine)
- Diagnostic workspace that surfaces DSM-5 criterion evidence and treatment-plan suggestions

A few ground rules we built in based on what the survey surfaced:

- Every suggestion shows its source so you can sanity-check it
- Nothing about which suggestions you accept or reject is ever sent to a supervisor
- You can turn any feature off and keep using SimplePractice the way you do now

What I'd ask from you:

- Use it on 5–10 real sessions over ~2 weeks
- A 20-minute check-in at the end so I can fix what's broken and catch what's missing
- Re-take the short Likert portion of the survey so we can measure whether it actually helped

Want in? Happy to do a 15-minute walkthrough whenever works.

— Anders

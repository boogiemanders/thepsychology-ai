# YC Pitch Updates: EaseVRx Precedent Framing

## Why this rewrite

EaseVRx (now RelieVRx) is the only FDA-cleared VR therapeutic. n=1,093, 12-month durability data. Ran on a breath sensor and a headset. No EEG. No HRV. That is the proven minimum-viable stack. Our pitch should treat them as the precedent we extend, not the competitor we beat. This is stronger because YC partners trust precedent.

Plus: the largest sham-controlled EEG NFB trial (n=116) failed to beat sham. Pitching EEG as Day 1 is a liability. Pitch eye tracking (86% depression accuracy is replicated) HARDER and EEG SOFTER.

## Interview talking points. Add these answers.

**"Why not just build EaseVRx clone?"**

"EaseVRx is pain-only and one-way. Their pipeline plays content at you for 8 weeks. We close the loop with breath, heart rate, and eye tracking. We extend the same FDA-cleared mechanism (breath biofeedback for pain) to anxiety, PTSD, autism, depression. Same engine, different content."

**"Why should we trust closed-loop biofeedback over EaseVRx's open-loop?"**

"EaseVRx is proof the breath component works. d = 0.40-0.49 vs sham. Our hypothesis: closed-loop adaptive content does even better. Adaptive VR doubles time-on-target vs static VR (Kritikos et al, arachnophobia RCT). Worth running the head-to-head."

**"Why not lead with EEG?"**

"EEG is roadmap not Day 1. Largest sham-controlled EEG NFB trial (n=116) failed to beat sham. Real-world cross-subject accuracy drops to 60-68 percent. Eye tracking, breath, and HRV are the proven backbone. EEG modules come once we have the patient data to fine-tune."

**"What is your unfair advantage now that EaseVRx exists?"**

"Three things. Closed-loop biofeedback they do not have. Eye tracking they cannot ship without an eye doctor on the team. Condition-agnostic engine they do not have because their FDA path locked them to pain. We build the platform. They built the first module."

## Why this is stronger

1. EaseVRx as precedent = de-risks the question "why hasn't this been built." Answer: it has, partially, and we extend it.
2. Pain-first launch = strongest evidence base (122 RCTs, 12-month durability data). Easier to defend than anxiety on call.
3. Eye tracking story stays untouched (it is the moat).
4. EEG de-emphasized = fewer attack surfaces in interview.
5. Closed-loop framing = clear, falsifiable, testable hypothesis.

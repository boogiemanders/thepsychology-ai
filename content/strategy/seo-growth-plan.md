**Sources:** Keyword research, competitive intel, Hormozi ($100M Leads), Priestley (Oversubscribed), site audit

## Executive Summary

thePsychology.ai has strong product-market fit ($20/mo vs $849+ competitors) and solid foundational content (8 blog posts, 81 topic guides, 12 domains). But the SEO surface area is thin compared to the opportunity. Competitors own the obvious keywords; we need to outflank them with volume, depth, and demand-creation tactics they can't replicate.

**The play:** Combine Hormozi's "give so much they feel guilty" content volume with Priestley's demand-signaling and scarcity — creating an SEO engine that both ranks AND converts.

## Priestley Demand-Creation Layer

These aren't separate content pieces — they're strategic overlays applied to everything above.

### 1. Replace "Sign Up" CTAs with Signal-Collecting CTAs
**Current:** "Start Free" / "Start Free Trial"
**New:** "Join the [Month] EPPP prep cohort — add your name to the interest list"

This creates demand-supply tension. Even if the product is always available, the *marketing* creates rhythm and urgency.

### 2. Timestamp and Window Content
Don't just publish "EPPP Pass Rates." Publish "Q2 2026 EPPP Pass Rate Analysis." Create freshness signals for Google AND psychological urgency.

### 3. 7-11-4 Content Architecture
Design content as a system of interlocking touchpoints:
- Landing on a blog post → leads to diagnostic quiz → leads to email with results → leads to sample lesson → leads to trial

Goal: 7 hours of engagement, 11 touchpoints, 4 different locations before someone converts.

### 4. Annual "State of the EPPP" Report
Data-driven report analyzing pass rates, score distributions, study method effectiveness. Becomes:
- The definitive citation source (backlink magnet)
- A campaign moment (pre-registration, release, follow-up)
- An authority signal for Dr. Chan

### 5. Coin Your Methodology
Name your study approach. When people search for it, only you rank. Competitors can't replicate branded search terms.

### 6. Visible Momentum
Add to content pages: "342 practice exams completed this week" / "1,200+ psychology candidates preparing with us." Make demand visible.

## Hormozi Conversion Architecture

### Lead Magnet Strategy (Give So Much They Feel Guilty)

**Primary lead magnet:** Free EPPP Diagnostic Quiz (25-30 questions)
- Show overall score ungated
- Email-gate the domain-by-domain breakdown with study recommendations
- This is a "reveal a problem" lead magnet — they see their weak spots and need your platform to fix them

**Secondary lead magnets:**
- Domain study guide PDFs (email-gated downloads)
- Study schedule templates (12-week, 8-week, 4-week)
- "50 Free EPPP Practice Questions with Explanations" (published on-page for SEO, extended set email-gated)

### 7-Email Nurture Sequence (After Lead Capture)

1. **Deliver + Bonus:** The promised asset + one bonus study tip
2. **Value:** "The #1 mistake EPPP candidates make in [their weak domain]"
3. **Social Proof:** "How [candidate profile] passed on the first attempt"
4. **Value:** "Your study schedule is probably wrong — here's why"
5. **Value:** "The 3 domains where most people lose points"
6. **Soft Pitch:** "How thePsychology.ai is different from AATBS/PsychPrep"
7. **Offer:** Trial, discount, or direct signup CTA

### Content-to-Lead Flow

Every SEO page should have ONE clear next step:
- Blog post about a domain → CTA: "Take a free 10-question diagnostic for this domain"
- Practice question page → CTA: "Get your score report emailed to you"
- Study guide → CTA: "Download the PDF + get a 3-day email crash course"
- Comparison page → CTA: "See how you'd score with AI-powered practice — try free"

### Keyword Intent → Conversion Strategy

| Intent Level | Keywords | Strategy |
|-------------|----------|----------|
| **Highest (ready to buy)** | eppp exam prep, eppp online course, affordable eppp prep | Sell directly — pricing, features, trial CTA |
| **Medium (evaluating)** | eppp practice tests, eppp sample questions, [competitor] review | Demonstrate quality — free samples, comparison, social proof |
| **Lowest (awareness)** | free eppp practice test, what is the eppp, eppp pass rate | Capture email — diagnostic quiz, guide download, newsletter |

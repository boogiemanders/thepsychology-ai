# Partnership Meeting Briefs

## Greg Inzinna

### Main goal

Leave the conversation with a **pilot partnership structure**, not a vague alliance.

### The deal shape

**thePsychology.ai**

- receives EPPP and early-career clinician traffic from Greg Inzinna
- converts that traffic into students and later providers
- optionally powers selected services for his company under a co-brand or "powered by" structure

**Greg Inzinna**

- gets a trusted EPPP prep destination for their audience
- gets a recruiting funnel for newly licensed clinicians
- gets access to platform services without needing to build them from scratch

### Simple way to explain it

> I want this to work like a closed-loop partnership. You can send trainees and early-career clinicians to thePsychology.ai for EPPP prep. We help them pass and stay in the ecosystem. Then, once they are licensed, we can route qualified clinicians back into your practice or network. If there are shared services that make sense, we can also do a powered-by or co-branded version, while the core platform stays with thePsychology.ai.

### What to ask Greg Inzinna for

- homepage or nav placement if relevant
- dedicated landing page or referral page
- webinar or email access to his audience
- agreement on who counts as a qualified clinician referral back to Greg Inzinna's practice or network
- agreement on pilot metrics

### What to offer Greg Inzinna

- custom landing page for his audience
- referral tracking
- co-branded education or content
- post-licensure clinician funnel back to his practice
- access to existing platform services

### What not to agree to live

- exclusivity
- equity
- shared ownership of IP
- unlimited free custom work
- permanent free product development

### Default commercial rule

**Shared platform access can be strategic. Custom work is billed.**

### Good pilot structure

Pilot length:

- 60 to 90 days

Pilot metrics:

- EPPP leads sent
- signup rate
- paid conversion rate
- number of licensure-stage users
- number of clinician candidates referred back to Greg Inzinna's practice or network

### Best next document after the call

- one-page partnership summary
- referral and co-marketing agreement draft
- short list of what is included vs custom

## NYU

### Main goal

Leave the conversation with a **research collaboration path**, not a general "let's work together sometime."

### The frame

You are not mainly asking NYU for business partnership.

You are asking for:

- research design rigor
- institutional credibility
- pilot and validation support
- publication pathway
- possible RA or student involvement

### Simple way to explain it

> I'm building a psychology lifecycle platform that starts with EPPP prep and expands into provider tools, assessment, and AI-assisted care. The near-term business wedge is licensure prep and provider growth. The near-term research opportunity is validation: learning science, digital assessment, and structured therapy-support tools. I'd like NYU to be a research and validation partner, not just an informal advisor.

### What to ask NYU for

- advice on the first study that is most publishable and realistic
- faculty sponsor or lab fit
- IRB path
- help shaping a validation roadmap
- possible student or RA support
- feedback on whether to start with EPPP outcomes, assessment validation, or therapy-support evaluation

### What to offer NYU

- a live platform with telemetry
- consent and de-identified research infrastructure
- a real applied environment
- data for publishable questions
- possible co-authorship and poster/publication pipeline

### Best first study options

1. **EPPP prep and wellness**
   Study adaptive learning, active recall, and wellness intervention effects.

2. **Digital assessment validation**
   Build early evidence for executive functioning tools.

3. **Structured AI support**
   Evaluate between-session exercises or guided psychoeducation, not autonomous therapy.

### What not to agree to live

- equity
- broad institutional ownership of your platform
- exclusive rights
- undefined publication or data rights

### Best next document after the meeting

- one-page research collaboration memo
- first-study concept note
- draft list of data, publication, and authorship principles

## Legal checklist for counsel

Ask a lawyer to help you create a lightweight starter stack:

- referral agreement template
- co-marketing agreement template
- services agreement + statement of work
- advisor agreement
- contractor agreement
- IP assignment language
- MOU template
- data use agreement template

That gives you a repeatable system instead of renegotiating the company every time someone interesting shows up.

# The Release Protocol — Step by Step

Source: Ch 6 "Releasing Trapped Emotions". The full procedure for finding and releasing one trapped emotion.

## The 6 steps (overview)

```
1. Obtain permission
2. Establish a baseline
3. Determine if a trapped emotion exists
4. Determine which emotion it is
5. Release the trapped emotion
6. Confirm release
```

Loop steps 3-6 for each emotion. Most sessions release 4-10 emotions.

## Pre-session

- Quiet room, no music or TV.
- Subject seated or standing comfortably.
- Practitioner state: love for subject, gratitude, openness, neutrality.

---

## Step 1 — Obtain Permission

- Working on yourself: skip.
- Working on another: "Can I have your permission to test you for trapped emotions?" Get a verbal yes or clear nod. **Do not muscle-test for permission**, the conscious mind must consent.

For minors / unconscious / animals → see [[05-surrogate-testing]] for who can consent.

## Step 2 — Establish a Baseline

Pick a muscle testing method (see [[02-muscle-testing]]).

Confirm testability:
- True statement: "My name is [real name]" → should test **strong / forward**.
- False statement: "My name is [other name]" → should test **weak / backward**.

If unclear, retry. If still unclear → untestable (water, adjustment, or surrogate).

## Step 3 — Does a Trapped Emotion Exist?

Ask: **"Do I (you) have a trapped emotion that can be released now?"**

| Response | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Strong / forward | Yes, proceed |
| Weak / backward | One of three things: (a) no trapped emotions (unlikely), (b) subconscious doesn't want to release now, (c) Heart-Wall is hiding access |

If no → either come back later or check for [[04-heart-wall]].
If yes → continue.

## Step 4 — Identify the Emotion (the binary funnel)

Goal: narrow 60 emotions → 1 in 4-5 binary questions, using the chart from [[01-the-chart]].

### 4a. Column

Ask: **"Is this trapped emotion in Column A?"**
- Yes → it's in Column A.
- No → it's in Column B. (Optionally double-check by asking Column B and getting yes.)

### 4b. Odd or even row?

Ask: **"Is this trapped emotion in an odd-numbered row?"**
- Yes → Row 1, 3, or 5.
- No → Row 2, 4, or 6.

### 4c. Specific row

For odd-row case: ask Row 1, then Row 3, then Row 5 until yes.
For even-row case: ask Row 2, then Row 4, then Row 6 until yes.

### 4d. Specific emotion

Now you have a 5-emotion cell. Test each emotion by name:
- "Is this trapped emotion **[name]**?" → strong = yes.
- One should test yes. If the first tests yes, double-check by testing the second (should test no).

### 4e. Optional digging

Once you have the emotion, ask: **"Do we need to know more about this trapped emotion?"**
- No → go to Step 5, release.
- Yes → narrow further:
  - When did it become trapped? (binary-search lifespan: before age 20? before 10? before 5?...)
  - In utero? Which trimester? (third trimester most common for absorbed-from-mother emotions)
  - Where in the body? (right/left/midline → above/below waist → narrow further)
  - What event? (subject thinks of an event, tester asks "is the trapped emotion about this event?")

You do **not** need to relive the event. Just confirm.

### Phrasing rules

- "**Is this trapped emotion [name]?**" works (question form).
- "**This trapped emotion is [name].**" works (statement form).
- Just saying the emotion word alone ("dread") may sway you backward from the negative word itself — bad signal. Always wrap in a statement or question.
- Pick statement or question and stick with it for the session.

---

## Step 5 — Release the Emotion

### On yourself
1. Place magnet between your eyebrows (touching skin or just over it).
2. Roll or slide the magnet **up the middle of the forehead, over the top of the head, down the back of the neck** as far as you can reach.
3. Repeat **3 times**.
4. Hold the intention: "release this trapped emotion."

Variations: lower spine works too. Hand works if no magnet. Any part of the Governing Meridian (head-to-tailbone midline) counts.

### On someone else
1. Place magnet on subject's back at base of neck.
2. Slide it down spine to lower back.
3. Lift, return to neck. Repeat. **3 strokes total.**
4. Hold the intention.

### Inherited emotion → 10 strokes, not 3

If the trapped emotion is inherited (passed at conception from a parent), apply **10 sweeps** instead of 3. Hold the intention to release from every ancestor and descendant that carries it. See [[04-heart-wall]] for Heart-Wall sweeps which are also 10.

---

## Step 6 — Confirm Release

Ask: **"Did I (we) release that trapped emotion?"**
- Yes → done. Loop back to Step 3 for the next one.
- No → repeat Step 5 with more focus, more love/gratitude, more belief.

Releases are permanent. A specific trapped emotion, once released, doesn't return.

---

## Targeted vs general

If a specific symptom is bugging you, instead of "do I have a trapped emotion that can be released now?" ask **specific**:

- "Is there a trapped emotion contributing to my back pain?"
- "Is there a trapped emotion preventing me from losing weight?"
- "Do I have a trapped emotion about my [husband/wife/boss/event]?"

This lets the subconscious surface emotions it might not otherwise offer up.

---

## Session limits

- 4-10 emotions per session is typical.
- After hitting the cap, ask: **"Will I be able to release another trapped emotion in [10 min / 2 hours / tomorrow]?"** to find when next.
- Healing happens in layers — same issue may need multiple sessions.

## Failure modes (book-flagged)

- **Projecting**: expecting an answer, body confirms whatever you wanted to hear. Stay neutral.
- **Resonance**: tester's own trapped emotion making the same emotion appear in every client. Fix: tester releases their own version.
- **Asking "did abuse happen to X?"**: explicitly off-limits. Muscle testing isn't reliable enough; risk of harm too high.

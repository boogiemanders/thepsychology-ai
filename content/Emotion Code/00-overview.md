# Emotion Code — Overview

Source: *The Emotion Code* by Dr Bradley Nelson (St Martin's Press, 2019). All distillations are for internal reference, not redistribution.

## Core premise

- Intense emotions can become "trapped" in the body when not fully processed.
- A trapped emotion is conceived as a **ball of energy**, baseball-sized to volleyball/cantaloupe-sized, lodged somewhere in the body.
- Trapped emotions distort the body's local energy field and over time can cause pain, malfunction, mood symptoms.
- The subconscious mind knows every trapped emotion: when it formed, where it lives, what event caused it.
- Releasing a trapped emotion is fast (often <1 minute) and permanent. Once released, it never returns.

## Vocabulary

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Trapped emotion | Ball of unprocessed emotional energy lodged in the body |
| Inherited trapped emotion | Trapped emotion passed at conception from a parent (and back through generations) |
| Heart-Wall | Multiple trapped emotions stacked into a protective shell around the heart. ~93% of people have one |
| Sway Test | Stand still, ask yes/no, body sways forward (yes) or backward (no) — the body as muscle test |
| Muscle test | Any kinesiology technique reading the subconscious yes/no signal (arm test, leg length, finger test, sway, etc) |
| Surrogate testing | Subject is present but untestable (child, animal, unconscious). Tester reads signal off a third person or self, within ~6 ft of subject |
| Proxy testing | Subject is remote. Proxy (self or third person) takes on subject's energy via verbal name-connection, gets tested in subject's place |
| Governing Meridian | Energy channel running up the spine and over the head to the upper lip. The release site |
| Magnet | A regular magnet (any kind) used to amplify intention. A hand or finger works as substitute |

## Scope and limitations (per the book itself)

- The book explicitly says muscle testing **must not** be used to diagnose disease or replace medical care.
- Releases are made of belief + intention; results can be subjective and influenced by projection.
- Never use muscle testing to "discover" abuse history that the subject hasn't disclosed. Stated as ethically dangerous.
- Don't run releases on someone without **explicit permission** (verbal okay or guardian consent for kids/animals/unconscious).
- Most people release 4-10 trapped emotions per session. Body limits how much it'll process at once.
- "Processing reactions" (mood dip, tiredness, dreams) can last 24-48h after a release.

## How this maps to the VR build

- The sway test = the core signal. Headset 6DOF replaces a separate observer.
- The chart = the decision tree. 60 emotions, narrowed to 1 via 4 binary questions.
- The release ritual = magnet/hand sweep down the Governing Meridian. 3 sweeps for trapped, 10 for inherited or Heart-Wall.
- The session log = standard practitioner habit; recorded for trend analysis.

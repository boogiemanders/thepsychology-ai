# Winning TikTok Hooks

Founder-approved spoken-hook templates for talking-head scripts (EPPP exam, strategy, and pop-culture lanes). Fill the brackets with the specific outcome, solution, or concept the video teaches. Pick the one that genuinely fits; do not force a mismatch. Spoken "EPPP" = "E triple P". No em dashes, no emojis.

- In 60 seconds I'm going to logically prove to you how you can literally [outcome].
- Here's why you should [solution].
- Here's how to [outcome]. Stop [solution, if it is about quitting something].
- Here's the ONE [solution] I used to [outcome].
- Like, [personal outcome] literally just from spamming this ONE [solution].
- Today, we're gonna be talking about how to [outcome].
- Alright, this is the single easiest way to [outcome].
- If you want to instantly [outcome], this is the ONLY video you'll ever need.
- Here's exactly how you're gonna [outcome] in 2026. You're gonna [solution].
- Here's the only video you'll ever need to watch to [outcome].
- Here's the strategy I used to [outcome]. I used a strategy I like to call [solution].
- The easiest way to [outcome] is to [solution].
- Is it possible to [crazy outcome] in X hours/days?
- POV: you and bro [something relatable].

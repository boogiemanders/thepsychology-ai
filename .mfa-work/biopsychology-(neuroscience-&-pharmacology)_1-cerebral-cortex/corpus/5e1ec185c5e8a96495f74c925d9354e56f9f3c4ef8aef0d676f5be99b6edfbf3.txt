Three types typically result from left (dominant) parietal damage: Type, Description, ExampleLimb-kinetic, Can't make precise, coordinated movements with fingers, hands, arms, or legs, Can't perform fine motor tasks like buttoning a shirt Ideomotor, Can't imitate movements or perform them on command, Can't pretend to comb hair when asked, even though they can do it spontaneously Ideational, Can't plan and execute sequential tasks, Can't complete the steps needed to make a sandwich in the right order

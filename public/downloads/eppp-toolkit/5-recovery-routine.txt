THE RECOVERY ROUTINE
What to do when you bomb a practice exam.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

A bad score is not a signal to panic.
It is a signal to run this routine.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 1: STOP (10 minutes)

Close the laptop. Walk away. Get water.
You cannot analyze clearly when your nervous system
is telling you that you've failed.

You have not failed. You took a practice test.
That is literally what practice is for.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 2: SCORE IT — WRITE THE NUMBER DOWN

  My score: ___/___  ( ___% )

Do not round up. Do not add caveats.
The number is neutral information.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 3: OPEN YOUR MISTAKE LOG

Go through every wrong answer. For each one, mark:

  [ ] Content Gap — I didn't know this
  [ ] Misread — I knew it but read the question wrong
  [ ] Trap — I knew it but picked the distractor

Do not skip any. Every miss gets categorized.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 4: FIND THE #1 PATTERN

Look at your log. Answer one question:

  "My biggest problem today was: _______________"

  Examples:
  - "I had 8 content gaps in Treatment/Intervention"
  - "I misread 6 questions — I keep skipping the word 'EXCEPT'"
  - "I fell for 5 trap answers in Ethics"

One pattern. Not three. Not "everything."

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 5: STUDY THAT ONE PATTERN (30 minutes)

  If Content Gap → Study that specific content.
                    Not the whole chapter. The specific concept.

  If Misread    → Re-read 5 missed questions slowly.
                  Circle the key word you skipped.
                  Practice: underline qualifiers (MOST, LEAST,
                  EXCEPT, BEST, PRIMARILY) before reading options.

  If Trap       → For each trap, write:
                  "I picked ___ because ___"
                  "The correct answer was ___ because ___"
                  "The difference is ___"

Set a timer. 30 minutes. Then stop.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 6: SCHEDULE YOUR 48-HOUR RETEST

Open your calendar right now and block time 48 hours from now
to retest the missed items.

  Retest scheduled for: ___________ at ___________

This is not optional. The retest is how you close the loop.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 7: REMIND YOURSELF

Read this out loud or in your head:

  "One bad practice score does not predict my exam result.
   I found my weak spots. I have a plan.
   The people who pass are not the ones who never struggle.
   They are the ones who have a system for what to do next."

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

WHEN TO USE THIS

- After any practice exam below 70%
- After any session where you feel demoralized
- After a long break when you feel behind
- Anytime you catch yourself thinking "I'll never pass"

The routine is the same every time.
That is the point.

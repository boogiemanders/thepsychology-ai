ACCREDITED DOCTORAL PROGRAMS FOR
TRAINING IN HEALTH SERVICE PSYCHOLOGY: 2025
This is the official listing of APA-accredited doctoral programs; it reflects all Commission on Accreditation (CoA)
decisions made in 2025 through October 18, 2025. The CoA has accredited doctoral programs in health service
psychology (i.e., clinical, counseling, school, and combinations thereof) offered by the institutions listed below.
The listing of programs accredited in 2025 includes the program’s name, area of health service psychology, degree
type, program address, date of initial accreditation, current accreditation status, and next site visit year. Program
review is governed by the Standards of Accreditation for Health Service Psychology (SoA). The standards for
evaluating programs can be obtained from the APA Accreditation website.

The CoA accepts third-party comment on programs in advance of their next scheduled review. For details on
providing comment, please refer to §4 of the Accreditation Operating Procedures (AOP) or by contacting the APA
Office of Program Consultation and Accreditation (OPCA) by telephone at (202) 336-5979 or by visiting the APA
Accreditation website.

Accreditation is a voluntary process. Programs that have not applied for and received accreditation are not included
in the following list. Programs that have applied for initial accreditation but not yet received a decision are listed on
the OPCA’s list of programs applying for initial accreditation while programs that have applied for and been noted
with the “intent to apply” designation are listed on the OPCA’s list of programs seeking intent to apply status.
Information about accreditation statuses and designations can be found in the AOP on the APA Accreditation
website.

The accreditation status listed for each program is reflective of its status as of October 18, 2025. If a review of a
program is not complete, the program’s prior accreditation status continues to be in effect until the review has been
completed and a final decision has been reached. 1 Programs listed as accredited and accredited, on contingency
have been judged by the CoA to be consistent substantively and procedurally with the SoA. Programs listed as
“accredited – inactive” have been given this status as the programs are not admitting trainees during the listed year.
Programs listed as “accredited, on probation” are, in the CoA’s judgment, not currently consistent with all aspects
of the SoA.

To obtain more information on the training aims and approaches of specific programs, the reader should contact
the programs directly.
